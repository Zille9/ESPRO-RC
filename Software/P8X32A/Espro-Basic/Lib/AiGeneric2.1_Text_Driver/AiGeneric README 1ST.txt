Included in the archive:

AiGeneric V2.1cf
--AiGeneric with Commodore .64c font loader

AiGeneric V2.1
--AiGeneric with Original font loader

FontGenerator10.zip by trodoss
--Included in AiGeneric V2.1

Both versions of AiGeneric support the following new commands:

	.redefine
	.putchar
	.pokechar
	.getchar
	.color
	.center

	and most other tv_text commands.
